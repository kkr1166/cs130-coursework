Kitra Razin
Kitra Razin is a Chicago-based composer and composition teacher. She has scored over 10 films, written chamber and solo concert music, and produced several original EDM and pop songs. In June 2020, she graduated from Northwestern University, where she double majored in Music Composition and Mathematical Methods in the Social Sciences.
